0/ Tagged Question:2015 is coming in 36 hours. What is the date one week from today in MM/DD/YYYY?

Tagged Answer:If 2015 is coming in 36 hours, then it is coming in 2 days. 2 days before 01/01/2015 is 12/30/2014, so today is 12/30/2014. So one week from today will be 01/06/2015. The answer is {01/06/2015}.
