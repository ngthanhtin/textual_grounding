0/ Tagged Question:A new building needed 11 windows. The builder had already installed 4 of them. If it takes 8 hours to install each window, how long will it take him to install the rest?

Tagged Answer:The building needs 11 windows, and the builder has already installed 4, leaving 7 more to go. Since each window takes 8 hours to install, he’ll need 7×8=56 hours to finish. The answer is {56}.

Tagged Answer:Mike invited 13 friends, but 7 couldn’t make it, so 6 friends came. If each gets 4 cupcakes, he should buy 6×4=24 cupcakes. The answer is {24}.

Tagged Answer:The painter has 12 rooms to paint, and he’s already done 5 rooms, so 7 rooms are left. At 7 hours per room, he’ll need 7×7=49 more hours. The answer is {49}.
3/ Tagged Question:While on vacation, Debby took 24 pictures at the zoo and 12 at the museum. If she later deleted 14 of the pictures, how many pictures from her vacation did she still have?

Tagged Answer:Debby took 24 pictures at the zoo and 12 at the museum, totaling 24+12=36 pictures. After deleting 14, she has 36−14=22 pictures left. The answer is {22}.

Tagged Answer:Robin started with 23 pieces of candy and ate 7, leaving 23−7=16 pieces. Her sister gave her 21 more, so now she has 16+21=37 pieces. The answer is {37}.
