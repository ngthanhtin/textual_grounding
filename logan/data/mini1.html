Question: Question: In the city of Soda, there are exactly 23786 inhabitants. They include 8417 men and 9092 women. The rest of the population is made up of children. How many kids are there in Soda?
Reformulated Question: In the city of Soda, there are exactly 23786 inhabitants. They include 8417 men and 9092 women. The rest of the population is made up of children. How many kids are there in Soda?

Answer:The total number of inhabitants is 23786. The total number of adults is the sum of 8417 men and 9092 women, which is 8417 + 9092 = 17509 adults. Therefore, the number of children is the total inhabitants minus the adults: 23786 - 17509 = **6277 kids**. The answer is **6277**.
