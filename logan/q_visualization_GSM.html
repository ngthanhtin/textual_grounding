These details will allow us to calculate the net elevation change and determine the highest point reached. ### Reformatted Text Question: Jo-Bob hopped into the hot air balloon, released the anchor rope, and pulled on the lift chain, which ignited the flame and provided the warm air that caused the balloon to rise. Releasing the chain for 10 minutes caused the balloon to descend 10*10=100 feet. The second 15-minute chain pull caused the balloon to rise another 50*15=750 feet.
